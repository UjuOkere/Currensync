PAYE Tax Guide for Nigerian Government Workers

This guide mirrors the Personal Income Tax Calculator provided by the Presidential Fiscal Policy & Tax Reforms Committee – comparing current and proposed PAYE tax bands under Nigeria’s new tax bill.

1. How to Use It

Enter your gross annual income (₦) to see a side-by-side comparison of your PAYE tax under both current and proposed laws. Note: Actual payable tax may be lower if you benefit from tax-deductible contributions such as pension or National Housing Fund.
