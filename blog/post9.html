Destiny Etiko Files ₦100M Lawsuit Over Defamation

By CurrenSync.vip | Updated July 5, 2025

Nollywood star Destiny Etiko has filed a pre-action legal notice seeking ₦100 million in damages from colleague Queeneth Hilbert.

The suit follows allegations that Etiko “snatched” Hilbert’s boyfriend—a claim branded defamatory in an official letter citing the Instagram outburst and demanding public retraction within 48 hours.

Etiko’s legal team says they’ll escalate to court if Hilbert fails to comply, declaring the claims "false and malicious." The dispute continues, capturing Nollywood’s attention.
