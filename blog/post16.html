🇳🇬 Nigeria @ 65: President Tinubu Calls for Unity, Hope, and Renewal

On this historic October 1st, Nigeria marks 65 years of independence. President Bola Ahmed Tinubu addressed the nation with a passionate call for unity, resilience, and renewed patriotism.

“We are a nation blessed with talent, diversity, and courage. At 65, our journey has not been without challenges, but we have remained standing as one people,” Tinubu said in his Independence Day broadcast.

The President highlighted ongoing reforms aimed at stabilizing the economy, empowering youth, and strengthening democratic institutions. He urged Nigerians to rise above ethnic, religious, and political divisions, describing unity as the country’s greatest weapon for progress.

He also paid tribute to Nigeria’s founding fathers and armed forces, reminding citizens that the sacrifices of the past should inspire renewed dedication to the country’s future. “Let us move forward together, hand in hand, building a Nigeria that works for all of us,” he concluded.

As Nigeria turns 65, the call is clear: unity, resilience, and hope for a brighter tomorrow. Happy Independence Day, Nigeria! 🇳🇬
