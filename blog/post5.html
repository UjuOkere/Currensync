Trump’s Tough Policies Hit Legal and Diplomatic Snags

By CurrenSync.vip | Updated July 2, 2025

Donald Trump is facing legal and diplomatic turbulence as courts and foreign allies push back against his latest policies.

A U.S. federal judge blocked Trump’s controversial asylum ban, calling it unconstitutional and in violation of international treaties. The move halts the administration's plan to immediately deport undocumented migrants at the southern border.

Simultaneously, Trump made a surprise announcement during a speech in Saudi Arabia, lifting sanctions on Syria and offering diplomatic negotiations with Iran. While some praised the decision as an “olive branch,” critics called it inconsistent and potentially dangerous.

As Trump’s team navigates legal hurdles at home and strategic tensions abroad, analysts say the former president’s legacy could hinge on how he handles this delicate balance.
