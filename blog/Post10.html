Fintech Funding Surges Past $2.2B in One Week

By CurrenSync.vip | Updated July 5, 2025

Fintech startups raised a whopping $2.24 billion this past week alone across 15 major funding rounds. Leading the charge were Stripe and Plaid, with large investments from global venture capital firms.

The trend highlights growing confidence in digital financial solutions, particularly in payments, lending, and decentralized platforms.
