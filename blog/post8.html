Queeneth hasn’t said much — but insiders say she’s gearing up with her own lawyers. One friend even hinted she has "receipts" that could blow the case wide open. Ewooo! Are we looking at Nollywood’s next courtroom reality show?

Whether this ends with an apology or a Netflix docuseries, one thing’s clear — Destiny’s not playing small. She came for the crown, and now she’s coming for her coins. 💅
