Bodies of Diogo Jota & Brother Arrive at Church in Portugal Ahead of Funeral

By CurrenSync.vip | Updated July 4, 2025

The tragic news has shaken football fans worldwide — the bodies of Diogo Jota and his brother André Silva arrived at the Igreja Matriz de Gondomar today, with family, friends, and fans gathering to pay their last respects ahead of the funeral on Saturday.

A wake is scheduled for Friday evening at the Capela da Ressurreição, followed by the funeral service at 10 a.m. local time. According to reports, Portuguese President Marcelo Rebelo de Sousa and former Portugal teammates will be present, with hundreds expected to line the streets to honor the late brothers.

Jota, aged 28, was traveling in Spain when their car reportedly experienced a tyre blowout, causing a fatal crash that claimed both brothers' lives. Jota had recently married and shared three young children.
